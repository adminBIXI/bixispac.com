Overview

Bitcoin Infrastructure Acquisition Corp (NASDAQ: BIXIU) is a special purpose acquisition company focused on bitcoin-native financial infrastructure and global stablecoin/payment platforms.

BIXI aims to identify and consummate a business combination with a leading company building on bitcoin and related payment rails, with target areas including custody, lending, stablecoins, payments, and other financial services that use bitcoin as a base layer.
