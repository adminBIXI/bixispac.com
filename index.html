Bitcoin Infrastructure Acquisition Corp

A special purpose acquisition company focused on bitcoin-native financial infrastructure and global stablecoin and payment platforms.

For official information, please refer to filings available through the SEC’s EDGAR system.

Overview

Bitcoin Infrastructure Acquisition Corp (“BIXI”) was formed to identify and complete a business combination with a leading company building on top of bitcoin and related payment infrastructure. Areas of focus include custody, lending, stablecoins, payments, and other financial services that leverage bitcoin as a base layer.
